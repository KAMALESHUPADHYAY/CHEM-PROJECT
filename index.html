What is weather forecasting?

Weather forecasting is the application of science and technology to predict the conditions of the atmosphere for a given location and time. It is important to human activities in many ways, including agriculture, transportation, and public safety.

Importance of Studiying weather forecasting

Weather forecasting is the application of science and technology to predict future weather conditions.
